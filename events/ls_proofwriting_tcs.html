Learners' Space - Proofwriting in Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science
June 27, 2024
This course dives into the fundamental structures that underpin modern mathematics, revealing the beauty and power of groups in solving complex problems.

Discover the elegance of mathematical reasoning in this immersive four-week course, where we unravel the core concepts of combinatorics, formal logic, elementary number theory, and proof techniques in general.

We will explore the rationale behind the development of proof-based mathematics and theoretical CS, and why as a student you should care about rigour in pure sciences. Some Extremal graph theory and Ramsey theory are also touched upon for a taste of industry computer science :)
